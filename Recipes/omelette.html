Omelette

A classic french dish

This is the simplest dish and also incredibly flexible depending on the ingrediends you choose to use. For this simple variation the chosen ingrediends are shown below

Ingredints

  • 2x Eggs
  • Mushrooms
  • Chives
  • Salt
  1. Heat up a frying pan with oil.
  2. Crack the eggs and mix the liquid.
  3. Add the mushrooms to the frying pan, after a few mins, throw in the eggs.
  4. Cook for 6 minutes in low heat and then add the chives and serve.
