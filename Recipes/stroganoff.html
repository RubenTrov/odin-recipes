Beef Stroganoff

A classic russian dish

The stroganoff sauce characteristic of this dish adds a savoury flavour.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Single cream
  • Sour cream
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Salt
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and garlic and add oil to the frying pan to heat it.
  2. Slice the beef in thin slices
  3. Add the pasta to a pot of boiling water and salt it
  4. Caramelise the onions on a fring pan with oil. Add the mushrooms as well.
  5. Add the beef to the frying pan mixture for 5 mins, stir the mixture
  6. Add the cream and sour cream to the frying pan, with som salt and pepper.
  7. Serve!
