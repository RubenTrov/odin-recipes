Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic italian dish

The bolognese sause characteristic of this dish adds a sweet savoury flavour. It is typical of Bologna, the city from which the sause takes its name.

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • mince beef
  • tomato sauce
  • salt
  • parmesan cheese
  • onion
  1. Dice the onion and garlic
  2. Put oil on the frying pan and heat it
  3. Add the diced onion to the pan and garlic and stir it
  4. Boil water on a pan and add spaghetti and salt to it
  5. Add the beef to the frying pan and mix it with the onion and garlic. Cook at meduim heat
  6. Add the tomato sauce and let it all simmer for a few minutes at meduim to low heat.
  7. Strain the sphagetti and mix it with the stuff in the frying pan.
  8. Grate some parmesan cheese on it and serve!
